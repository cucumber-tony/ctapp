Third Party Intergration

This section covers third party devices that are integrated with CT. If you are looking full firmware integration, please see the previous step.

Third party devices that can integrate with us are as follows:

  • Meraki
  • Ruckus ZD
  • Ruckus vSZ
  • MikroTik
  • Aruba
  • OpenWRT/OpenMesh

Select the box you have from above and follow the guide to integrate with it. Once you have done this, if you haven't already added your box, add it by following this guide.
