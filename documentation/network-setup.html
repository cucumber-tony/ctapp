Quick Network Setup

Sign Up

To get started with Cucumber Tony, head over to my.ctapp.io and once here, click Sign Up, enter your email address and then click on the Sign-up for free button.

You'll be sent a registration link by email, simply open it up and click on the link, which will start you on your account creation in Cucumber Tony.

Your First Location

You will now have to fill in a few bits of information, including things like your Location name and custom brand.

Just follow the steps in the setup one by one as shown above, until you reach your location dashboard.
