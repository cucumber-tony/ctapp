Quick Network Setup

To get started with Cucumber Tony, head over to www.ctapp.io. Once here, enter your email address into the field and click on Go.

You'll be sent a registration link by email, simply open it up and click on the link, which will start you on your account creation in Cucumber Tony.

Clicking the link will bring you to a page where you will need to set your company/location name, and then enter in a postal address and click Next.

The next step is to set up a custom brand. Your customer brand will be used to create a custom URL for you. Don't worry if you've given it a silly name, you can change this later.

Now give yourself a name and a secure password for your account and click Next.

This completes the sign up process. You'll now be taken to your account dashboard. As you've just added some details, Cucumber Tony has taken this and created a location for you. To customise and view your location, simply click on Customise your location.
