Common Questions

Are there any limits on the free plan?

Paid plans let you add unlimited access points and invite admin users. Plus you get a much higher quota of splash views.

How many guest users should I expect?

Monitor how many people use your guest Wi-Fi in your dashboard. A typical cafe in central London gets 1,000 splash impressions per month.

What's the difference between the plans?

Features and usage allowance. If you're using the splash pages there is a limit to the number of times this page can be viewed per month. Higher plans have higher usage allowances.

What if I go over my guest usage limit?

You'll get an email when you approach your limit. You should uppgrade if you're on the free account. If you're on a paid plan, we can apply a charge for the additional views. The charge is $3 per 10,000 views.

What is a splash page view?

If you're using the splash pages there's a limit to the number of times the pages can be viewed per month. Every time a guest user tries to connect they will see a splash page. We count how many hits there are to your splash page per month.

There is no limit on your private network as this doesn't use a splash page.

Can you manage the Wi-Fi network for me?

If you'd like dedicated support and management, please get in touch. Tell us a bit about your network and we'll see how we can help.

What's Top Up?

If you go over your monthly splash views, we'll charge you $3 per 10,000 impressions unless you upgrade.

What's QoS

QoS prioritises the jobs that are sent to your boxes. A job might be when you change your SSID or reboot your boxes.

If load is high, we'll processes priority jobs faster.

What are Integrations

Connect your other WebApps with Cucumber. Eg. automatically sync your guests with your Salesforce account or send an alert to your Slack account if a box goes offline.

What are Triggers

Triggers determine when a particular integration is called. A box reboot is a trigger.
