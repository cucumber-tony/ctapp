West London Venues

The Electric, Notting Hill

(191 Portobello Road, London, W11 2ED)

This Italian-American spot has a great setting, close to the station. The booths mean you can have a formal meeting or an all-day sit in. The décor is pristine making it perfect for impressing clients. The prices are steep but you can get many reasonable sides & drinks.

Wi-Fi: 4/5
Service: 2/5
Atmosphere: 4/5
Power Points: 4/5
Coffee/Menu: 3/5

The Imperial 577, Fulham

(191 Portobello Rd, Notting Hill, London W11 2ED)

This is a classic setting that has been renovated. The pricing is pretty steep but the drinks & small bites are reasonable. This is good for a few hours work, if you want calm and natural light it’s perfect. It mainly suits having Lunch or Dinner, but it’s lovely for a spring afternoon.

Wi-Fi: 2/5
Service: 4/5
Atmosphere: 2/5
Power Points: ?/5
Coffee/Menu: 4/5

Beaufort House, Chelsea

(354 Kings road, Chelsea SW3 5UZ)

This has meeting rooms and a brunch menu that's popular, upscale and perfect to bring someone for a meeting. The mood is more diners than people on Laptops. However you can book meeting rooms so it's ideal for groups.

Wi-Fi: 2/5
Service: 4/5
Atmosphere: 2/5
Power Points: ?/5
Coffee/Menu: 4/5

Paradise By Way of Kensal Green

(19 Kilburn Ln, London W10 4AE)

This is an upscale gastro-diner that prides itself on British cuisine. The pricing is pretty mid-range. It’s perfect to escape the busy streets of London; it has outdoor seating that has a lovely setting where you could sit all day.

Wi-Fi: 2/5
Service: 4/5
Atmosphere: 4/5
Power Points: ?/5
Coffee/Menu: 4/5

Joe And The Juice

(65-67 Broadwick Street, W1F 9QY)

Situated in the heart of London, this is perfect for avoiding the crowds and seeking some solace. They have a different branch with cozy sofas. It’s small so can get busy quickly but it has amazing coffee and small lunches.

Wi-Fi: 5/5
Service: 4/5
Atmosphere: 3/5
Power Points: 3/5
Coffee/Menu: 4/5

Minkies Deli

(Chamberlayne Rd, NW10 5RQ)

This Deli has a range of options for food & drink including a make-your-own Breakfast. The setting is light and spacious with a combination of outdoor and indoor seating. It's quiet enough to let you work in peace. You can also admire the street from inside.

Wi-Fi: 2/5
Service: 4/5
Atmosphere: 3/5
Power Points: 2/5
Coffee/Menu: 4/5

The Tea & Coffee Plant

(180 Portobello Rd, W11 2EB)

This small, yet cozy Café is perfect if you like your coffee. Try the Colombian fair trade coffee. It’s ideal for a few hours, but not somewhere to bring clients or large groups of people. They serve toasted sandwiches and an array of brilliant snacks.

Wi-Fi: 2/5
Service: 4/5
Atmosphere: 1/5
Power Points: 1/5
Coffee/Menu: 4/5

The Troubadour

(263-265 Old Brompton Rd, SW 9JA)

This is a historical place that has its trendy roots in the 1950’s Bohemian era, Bob Dylan played his first London gig here. You can hire out private rooms, and it's menu is reasonably priced. This is ideal for work meetings that can spill over into drinks, there is regularly live music so it’s best to go in the afternoon/morning if you're looking for calm, quiet surroundings.

Wi-Fi: 2/5
Service: 2/5
Atmosphere: 4/5
Power Points: 1/5
Coffee/Menu: 4/5

The Westbourne

(101 Westbourne Park Villas, W2 5ED)

This is perfect for late afternoon/evening meetings, its down to earth persona in the midst of Notting Hill means it has a reasonably priced menu, it can get busy on weekends. The setting is perfect to hold group meetings, and the staff are welcoming. The drinks range from Belgian beers to coffee, the menu changes daily and is made from fresh food from independent suppliers.

Wi-Fi: 2/5
Service: 4/5
Atmosphere: 3/5
Power Points: 2/5
Coffee/Menu: 4/5

Camden Arts Centre Café

(Arkwright Rd, NW3 6DG)

Time out describes this venue as ‘best coffee in West London’ so it’s perfect for coffee that will keep you energised throughout the day. It’s on the edge of Camden and you can work outside alongside artwork. There is also a gallery and their pastries are popular.

Wi-Fi: 2/5
Service: 4/5
Atmosphere: 3/5
Power Points: 3/5
Coffee/Menu: 5/5
