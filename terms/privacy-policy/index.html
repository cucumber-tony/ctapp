Information We Collect And Receive

Cucumber Tony may collect and receive Customer Data and other information and data ("Other Information") in a variety of ways:

Customer Data. Customers or individuals granted access to the Internet by a Customer ("Authorized Users") routinely login and submit Customer Data to Cucumber Tony when using the Services. This includes social profiles etc.

Other Information. Cucumber Tony also collects, generates and/or receives Other Information:

Dashboard and Account Information. To create or update a Dashboard account, you or your Customer (e.g., your employer) supply Cucumber Tony with an email address, phone number, password, domain and/or similar account details. In addition, Customers that purchase a paid version of the Services provide Cucumber Tony (or its payment processors) with billing details such as credit card information, banking information and/or a billing address.

Usage Information

Log data. As with most websites and technology services delivered over the Internet, our servers automatically collect information when you access or use our Websites or Services and record it in log files. This log data may include the Internet Protocol (IP) address, the address of the web page visited before using the Website or Services, browser type and settings, the date and time the Services were used, information about browser configuration and plugins, language preferences and cookie data.

Device information. Cucumber Tony collects information about devices accessing the Services, including type of device, what operating system is used, device settings, application IDs, unique device identifiers and MAC addresses. Whether we collect some or all of this Other Information often depends on the type of device used and its settings.

Location information. We receive information from you, your Customer and other third-parties that helps us approximate your location. For example, we know the address of the business from where you access the Services or an IP address received from your browser or device to determine approximate location.

Cookie Information. Cucumber Tony uses cookies and similar technologies in our Websites and Services that help us collect Other Information. The Websites and Services may also include cookies and similar tracking technologies of third parties, which may collect Other Information about you via the Websites and Services and across other websites and online services. For more details about how we use these technologies, please see our Cookie Policy.

Networking DataCucumber Tony collects data from devices running the Cucumber Tony firmware. Networking data includes information like data transferred, client mac addresses and signal strength. This data is cached for 30 days in a data storage facility located within the EU. We use this data to improve the quality of service.

WiFi AnalyticsCucumber Tony may collect WiFi analytics and environmental data from various sources. This data is not used for location analytics, user tracking. This data is used, like the networking data, to improve network performance. Client identifiers are encrypted with a random salt unique to the Customer that gets refreshed every 30 days. Data is expired after 30 days automatically.

Additional Information Provided to Cucumber Tony. We receive Other Information when submitted to our Websites or if you participate in a focus group, contest, activity or event, apply for a job, request support, interact with our social media accounts or otherwise communicate with Cucumber Tony.

Where Is My Data?

Customer and User data is primarily stored in Google Cloud platform in their European data centres. Data is encrypted at rest. Data including Email addresses is processed (but not stored) in the US since that's where Google's Cloud Functions run. Backups of the data are encrypted and stored in the EU.
